Capture your experiences on the go.

With our free Mobile Apps you can capture, edit and share your life whenever you are out and about. From city trips, holidays or favourite Pub tours to biking, walking, hiking or running, Maptales is the easiest way to map your experiences using geo location information.

Enrich your experience with your pictures and waypoints that matter for you.

Take geo tagged pictures or post a comment along the way. These will be linked to your experience instantly and automatically. On the way we will recommend you relevant waypoints. Add them to your experience and pop-in to check-out. Your waypoint might be the “Art Gallery” while others get the “Tattoo Shop” next to it. It’s all based on people who have had similar experiences like you.

Explore experiences around you or monitor your favourite places

Maptales is a great way to explore experiences around you or from around the world using our ‘square of interest’ experience feeds. Simply pick an experience and explore it using a map or in 3D via Augmented Reality overlays. Follow the trail and explore relevant waypoints such as sight seeing spots or Shops that mattered to people with similar interests to you.
