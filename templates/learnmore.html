Mobile Apps

With our free Mobile Apps you can capture, edit and share your life whenever you are out & about, alone or as a group. From city trips, holidays or favourite Pub tours to biking, walking, hiking or running, Maptales is the easiest way to map your experiences using geo location information.

Collaborate with Friends

Telling Stories alone can be boring. With Maptales your Friends can create Experiences with you.

Import from everywhere

Do you prefer using Twitter for Microblogging, Flickr for you Images? No problem, just import your content from external sources.

Export your Stories

You can export your Stories to Facebook, your Blog etc.

Find Experiences around You

Enrich your experience with recommended waypoints that matter to you. On the way we will recommend relevant waypoints, based on what you currently want to do. Your suggested waypoint might be the “Art Gallery” while others get the “Tattoo Shop” next to it. It’s all based on what you are interested in and information from people who have had similar experiences to you.

Do you like it?

Help us decide which experiences are worth displaying and which are not. How? Just like/dislike them.

Square of Interest

Explore experiences around you or monitor your favourite topics and places. Simply pick an experience and explore it using a map or via Augmented Reality overlays. Follow the trail and explore relevant waypoints such as sightseeing spots or shops that mattered to people with similar interests to you.

Location, Location, Location ...

Our goal is to help you find the good activities/locations/people around you. The more you post the higher your rank gets in a certain area.

Privacy

You have full control over your privacy. You can create Stories for Friends, Followers, Registered Users or even make them publicly available
